(ENG)
Description: The work consists of developing an e-commerce website, using only JavaScript, HTML and CSS technologies. 
The use of any framework that assists in the development of the work is not permitted. The work will be developed individually. 
You can seek help from tutorials on the internet, but work copied from the internet or from colleagues will not be accepted. 
The work must contain at least the following characteristics:

• 4 (four) pages linked together;
• 1 Table – 0.5 points;
• 1 List – 0.5 points;
• Images, Audios and Videos – 1.0 points;
• 1 Form with at least 6 fields of different types – 1.0 point;
• Styling and pleasant layout of pages with CSS – 3.0 points;
• Responsive Layout for at least two devices – 1.0 point;
• Add 6 different events with JS – 3.0 points.
